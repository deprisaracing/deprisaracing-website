About Us

Learn about our mission, achievements, and the passion that drives us forward

What is STEM Racing?

STEM Racing is a fun and fast-paced competition where students team up to design, build, and race a mini CO‚ÇÇ-powered car. It blends science, technology, engineering, and math with real-world tools, while building teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving skills for future careers.

Who Are We?

We are the new generation of innovators at DepriSa Racing Team, SMK Tun Mutahir, Batu Berendam, Melaka, driven by our passion for STEM and the thrill of competition.

Why "DepriSa"?

Our team name "DepriSa" is a fusion of two powerful concepts: "Depression" representing the aerodynamic low-pressure zones we create for speed, and "Sa" from our school's name. Together, they symbolize our innovative approach to racing and our school pride.
