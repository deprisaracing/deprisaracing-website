About Us

Learn about our mission, achievements, and the passion that drives us forward

What is STEM Racing?

STEM Racing is a fun and fast-paced competition where students team up to design, build, and race a mini CO₂-powered car. It blends science, technology, engineering, and math with real-world tools, while building teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving skills for future careers.

Who Are We?

We are the new generation of innovators at DepriSa Racing Team, SMK Tun Mutahir, Batu Berendam, Melaka, driven by our passion for STEM and the thrill of competition.

Why "DepriSa"?

Our team name "DepriSa" is a fusion of two powerful concepts: "Depression" representing the aerodynamic low-pressure zones we create for speed, and "Sa" from our school's name. Together, they symbolize our innovative approach to racing and our school pride.

Project Objective

We are currently seeking funding to support the development of a high-performance miniature Formula 1 racing car. Our goal is to design, build, refine, and race an advanced car that highlights innovation, cutting-edge engineering, and technology.

Key Objectives:

  • Design and build a competitive and innovative F1 racing car
  • Test and optimize the racing car's performance
  • Compete in STEM RACING MALAYSIA STATE-LEVEL COMPETITION 2025

This project represents an incredible opportunity for our team to apply STEM principles in a practical, exciting context while developing valuable skills in engineering, project management, and teamwork.

×

Expected Results

Our participation in the STEM Racing Malaysia competition will yield significant benefits beyond the race track. We anticipate the following outcomes:

  • Successfully designed, built, and assembled a functional racing car
  • Gained valuable exposure and experience through participation in the STEM Racing Malaysia State-Level Competition 2025
  • Elevated the image of both the school and the state of Melaka, earning wider recognition
  • Established a strong and respectable reputation for the team, the school, and Melaka
  • Enhanced engineering skills and significantly improved teamwork among team members

These outcomes will not only benefit our current team members but will also create a legacy for future students interested in STEM fields.
